TwoGo (Delivery application for users who want to split meal costs)

Features
	Delivery system / menu
	Restaurant information
	Food information
	Price of delivery [minimum]
	Ratings
	Service time
	Popular order sets for individual / group eating
Table view of orders (made by other users)
	Drop down list of updating requests
	Filter of specific food types
	Price range of delivery
	Time until order is made
	Users needed to make order
	Location of meeting room / location

Modal view
	Join ordering group
		Method of payment
		Agree to current order or add more meals
		Suggest different meeting location
	Make new order
		Type of food user wants to order
		Individually eating or eating as a group
		Where to meet to eat the food
		Method of payment
