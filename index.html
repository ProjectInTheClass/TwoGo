TwoGo

TwoGo is a delivery application for users who want to share the cost of a meal without having to pay extra to reach the minimum price

  • Learn more

TwoGo tab

The TwoGo tab shows rooms made by users searching for people to share a meal with. By showing information such as the time until the order is made, the minimum amount of money each user will have to pay, and the room settings, users can choose which room they want to join at a single glance.

  • Learn more

Payment tab

Users can see the meals they have to pay for and confirm that the hosts of the rooms they joined have been compensated.

History tab

Users can see their previous meals through the history tab so they can re-order certain deliveries they've ordered before.

Profile tab

Settings regarding the application, user preferences, location settings, and more can be found in the profile tab.
